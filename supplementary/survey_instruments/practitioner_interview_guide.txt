QALIS PRACTITIONER INTERVIEW GUIDE
============================================================

Version: 1.0 | Approved by: Research Ethics Board
Expected duration: 50-80 minutes

[IC1] Production Deployment Experience
----------------------------------------
Can you walk me through the LLM-integrated systems you've deployed in production? What were the primary use cases and what LLM provider did you use?

Follow-up probes:
  - Can you give a specific example?
  - How did you detect/measure this?
  - What was the business impact?
  - How have you addressed this?

[IC2] Quality Challenges
----------------------------------------

  - What was the business impact?
  - How have you addressed this?
